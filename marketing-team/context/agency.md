## What We Do

5 Cypress Automation operates two service lines that often work together:

### 1. Marketing Services (this system)
We act as an embedded AI marketing team for clients who need strategy, execution, and reporting but can't afford or don't want a full in-house marketing department.
- **Strategy briefs & roadmaps** — Market research, competitive analysis, positioning, 90-day plans
- **Email campaigns** — Nurture sequences, outreach, re-engagement, onboarding
- **Social media content** — LinkedIn posts, content calendars, thought leadership
- **Reports & dashboards** — Performance reporting, KPI dashboards, campaign wrap-ups
- **Presentations** — HTML presentations, one-pagers, QBR decks

### 2. Automation Services
We build backend automation workflows that eliminate manual work and connect business tools.
- **Sales → QuickBooks** — Forms to invoices with inventory checks and payment
- **ShipStation integration** — Auto-create and track shipping orders from sales
- **SEO machine** — Local SEO audits, outreach, monthly optimization retainers
- **Lead generation** — Scraping, research, scoring, and delivery to Google Sheets
- **Missed-call text-back** — Local SMB quick-win: $500 setup + $97/mo
- **Document generation** — AI-generated proposals, contracts, SOWs as branded PDFs
- **Monthly insights** — Automated reporting from CRM/automation metrics
