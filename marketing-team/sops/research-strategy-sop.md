# SOP: Market Research & Strategy Brief
# 5 Cypress Automation — Standard Operating Procedure

---

## Purpose
This SOP defines the exact process for producing a Marketing Research & Strategy Brief for a 5 Cypress client. The output is a polished, client-ready document that gives the client a clear picture of their market landscape and a concrete 90-day marketing roadmap.

---

## When to Use This SOP
- New client onboarding (first strategy deliverable)
- Quarterly strategy refresh
- New product or service launch
- Entering a new market or audience segment

---

## Inputs Required Before Starting
Before executing this SOP, confirm you have:
- [ ] Client context file (`marketing-team/context/[client-name].md`) filled out
- [ ] Agency context (`context/agency.md`) loaded
- [ ] Access to web research (Perplexity MCP or web search)
- [ ] Any existing materials the client has shared (deck, one-pager, past campaigns)

---

## Output Format
Deliver a single Markdown document saved to:
`marketing-teammarketing-team/output/[client-name]/strategy-brief-[YYYY-MM].md`

---

## Process: Step by Step

### Phase 1 — Context Intake (5 min)
1. Read `marketing-team/context/agency.md`
2. Read `marketing-team/context/[client-name].md`
3. Identify: What is the client's core offer? Who is the buyer? What's the primary goal?
4. Note any gaps in the client context file — flag these at the end of the deliverable

### Phase 2 — Market Research (15-20 min)
Use Perplexity MCP or web search to research the following. Document findings with sources.

**2a. Industry Landscape**
- Current state of the client's industry (trends, growth, regulatory environment if medical)
- Key shifts happening in the next 12-24 months
- Market size and opportunity (approximate is fine)

**2b. Target Audience Research**
- What are buyers in this space actively searching for / concerned about?
- Common objections or hesitations in the buying process
- Language and terminology buyers use (for messaging alignment)

**2c. Competitive Analysis**
- Research 3-5 named competitors (use client context file for names, or identify from search)
- For each competitor note: positioning, key messages, apparent strengths, apparent weaknesses
- Identify gaps or underserved angles in the competitive landscape

**2d. Channel & Tactic Benchmarks**
- What marketing channels are most effective in this industry/audience?
- Any benchmark data on email open rates, conversion rates, or CAC for this space

### Phase 3 — Positioning & Messaging Synthesis (10 min)
Based on research, draft:
- **Positioning Statement:** One paragraph defining how this client should position against competitors given the market
- **3 Core Messages:** The 3 most compelling things this client should consistently communicate
- **Value Proposition:** One crisp sentence that captures the unique value they deliver

### Phase 4 — 90-Day Marketing Roadmap (10 min)
Build a concrete, prioritized plan organized by month:

**Month 1 — Foundation**
- Priority actions (max 4)
- Quick wins to execute immediately

**Month 2 — Activation**
- Campaigns or initiatives to launch
- Channels to activate or test

**Month 3 — Optimization**
- What to measure and optimize
- How to build on what's working

For each action item include:
- What it is
- Why it matters (strategic rationale)
- Who owns it (client vs. 5 Cypress)
- Success metric

### Phase 5 — KPIs & Success Metrics
Define 4-6 KPIs that matter for this client's goals. For each:
- Metric name
- Current baseline (if known)
- 90-day target
- How it will be tracked

## Quality Checklist Before Delivering
- [ ] Rooted in actual research, not generic advice
- [ ] Specific to this client's industry and audience
- [ ] Roadmap actions are concrete and actionable (not vague)
- [ ] KPIs are measurable and realistic
- [ ] Formatted cleanly and ready to send to a client
- [ ] Saved to correct output folder
