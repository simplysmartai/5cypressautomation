## 1. Purpose

The parties wish to explore a potential business relationship regarding {{project_description}} ("Purpose"). In connection with this Purpose, Disclosing Party may share confidential information with Receiving Party.

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by Disclosing Party that:
- Is marked as confidential, or
- Should reasonably be understood to be confidential given its nature

This includes but is not limited to:
- Business processes and strategies
- Customer data and lists
- Technical systems and architecture
- Financial information
- Product plans and roadmaps
- Credentials and access information

## 3. Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that:
- Is or becomes publicly available without breach
- Was known to Receiving Party before disclosure
- Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information
- Is disclosed by a third party without restriction

## 4. Obligations

Receiving Party agrees to:
- Use Confidential Information only for the Purpose
- Protect Confidential Information with reasonable care
- Limit access to personnel with a need to know
- Not disclose to third parties without prior written consent
- Promptly notify Disclosing Party of any unauthorized disclosure

## 5. Required Disclosures

Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information if required by law, provided:
- Prompt notice is given to Disclosing Party (where legally permitted)
- Reasonable assistance is provided to seek protective order
- Only the minimum required information is disclosed

## 6. Return of Information

Upon request or termination of discussions:
- Receiving Party will return or destroy all Confidential Information
- Receiving Party will certify destruction in writing if requested
- Residual memory in personnel minds is not a violation

## 7. No License or Obligation

- No license is granted under any patent, copyright, or other right
- Neither party is obligated to proceed with any business relationship
- This Agreement does not create any agency or partnership

## 8. Term

This Agreement is effective from the Effective Date and continues for {{term_years}} years, unless terminated earlier by mutual written agreement.

Obligations regarding Confidential Information survive termination for {{survival_years}} years.

## 9. Remedies

Receiving Party acknowledges that breach may cause irreparable harm. Disclosing Party is entitled to seek injunctive relief without proving actual damages.

## 10. General Provisions

### 10.1 Entire Agreement
This Agreement is the entire agreement regarding confidentiality.

### 10.2 Amendments
Amendments require written agreement signed by both parties.

### 10.3 Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of {{governing_law}}.

### 10.4 Severability
Invalid provisions do not affect remaining provisions.
