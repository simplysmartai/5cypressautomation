## 1. Services & Deliverables

Agency will provide the following services over a 30-day period:

### Phase 1 (Days 1-14): Foundation Setup
- Microsoft Forms integration with QuickBooks Online
- Automated invoice creation and delivery
- Semi-automated shipping order creation (with manual approval)
- Email confirmations to customers
- Basic inventory tracking (Google Sheets)
- Slack notifications for new orders

### Phase 2 (Days 15-30): Full Automation
- Remove manual approval step (direct-to-carrier automation)
- Real-time inventory integration
- Low stock alerts
- Address validation
- Failed order retry logic
- Complete testing and documentation

**Success Criteria:**
- 100% of test orders processed correctly
- Orders completed within 2 minutes of submission
- Zero data entry errors in automated flow

- 2 hours of operations team time for:
