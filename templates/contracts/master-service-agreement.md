# Master Service Agreement

**Between:** Simply Smart Automation LLC ("Agency")  
**And:** {{client_name}} ("Client")  
**Effective Date:** {{effective_date}}

---

## 1. Services

Agency agrees to provide automation consulting and development services ("Services") as described in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") executed under this Agreement.

## 2. Statements of Work

Each project will be governed by a separate SOW that references this Agreement. Each SOW will specify:
- Scope of work and deliverables
- Timeline and milestones
- Fees and payment schedule
- Project-specific terms

In case of conflict between this Agreement and an SOW, the SOW controls for that project.

## 3. Fees and Payment

### 3.1 Project Fees
Fees are specified in each SOW. Unless otherwise stated:
- 50% deposit due before work begins
- 50% final payment due upon completion

### 3.2 Payment Terms
Invoices are due within seven (7) days of receipt. Late payments incur 1.5% monthly interest.

### 3.3 Expenses
Pre-approved expenses (APIs, software, etc.) are billed at cost plus 10%.

## 4. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:
- Provide timely access to systems, accounts, and personnel
- Respond to requests within 2 business days
- Designate a single point of contact
- Provide feedback within agreed timeframes

Delays caused by Client may extend timelines and/or incur additional fees.

## 5. Intellectual Property

### 5.1 Client Owns Deliverables
Upon full payment, Client owns all custom work product created specifically for Client.

### 5.2 Agency Retains Tools
Agency retains ownership of:
- Pre-existing tools, frameworks, and methodologies
- Reusable components not specific to Client
- General knowledge and skills

Agency grants Client a perpetual license to use any Agency tools incorporated in deliverables.

### 5.3 Portfolio Rights
Agency may reference the project in marketing materials unless Client requests confidentiality.

## 6. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to protect confidential information disclosed during the engagement. This includes:
- Business processes and strategies
- Technical systems and credentials
- Financial information
- Customer data

Confidentiality survives termination for three (3) years.

## 7. Warranties and Limitations

### 7.1 Warranty
Agency warrants that Services will be performed professionally and deliverables will function as specified for 30 days after delivery.

### 7.2 Limitation of Liability
Agency's total liability is limited to fees paid for the specific SOW. Agency is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

### 7.3 No Guarantee of Results
While Agency strives for excellent outcomes, business results depend on many factors beyond Agency's control. Agency does not guarantee specific business outcomes.

## 8. Term and Termination

### 8.1 Term
This Agreement remains in effect until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.

### 8.2 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately if the other materially breaches and fails to cure within 15 days of written notice.

### 8.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- Client pays for work completed
- Agency delivers work product for paid work
- Both parties return confidential information

## 9. Independent Contractor

Agency is an independent contractor. Nothing creates an employment, partnership, or joint venture relationship.

## 10. Miscellaneous

### 10.1 Entire Agreement
This Agreement and any SOWs constitute the entire agreement.

### 10.2 Amendments
Changes require written agreement signed by both parties.

### 10.3 Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of [State/Jurisdiction].

### 10.4 Dispute Resolution
Disputes will be resolved through good-faith negotiation, then mediation, before litigation.
