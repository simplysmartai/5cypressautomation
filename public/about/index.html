The story

Why accounting
and finance firms?

I built automation systems for a few years across different industries — e-commerce, SaaS, professional services. The pattern I kept seeing was that the businesses with the most to gain were the ones where the work was highly repeatable, the data was in structured systems, and the people doing manual work were too expensive to be doing it.

Accounting firms are the clearest example of that pattern. Partners billing at $300–$500/hour are sending monthly report emails. Staff spending 3 hours on document sorting during tax season. Signed engagement letters sitting in someone's inbox waiting for 45 minutes of setup work.

The automations are not complicated. The APIs exist. The problem is that most firms don't have a developer, and most developers don't understand the accounting workflow well enough to build systems that actually fit. That gap is what 5 Cypress fills.

How we work

The rules we
build by.

No lock-in, ever

You own the code, the hosting account, and the API keys. If you decide to bring development in-house or stop working with us, we hand everything over and wish you well. There's no proprietary platform, no recurring license, nothing that holds you hostage.

Fixed price, scoped first

Every project is priced before a line of code is written. The scope document defines exactly what gets built, what doesn't, and what happens if requirements change. No hourly billing, no surprise invoices.

Official APIs only

We connect systems using their official APIs — not screen scrapers, browser extensions, or hacks that break when the UI changes. That means your automations are stable and not dependent on us staying on top of them.

Documented for the long run

Every system ships with a runbook: what the automation does, how to restart it if it stops, what to check when something looks wrong, and how to update configuration. Written for the office manager who will be maintaining it in two years.

We tell you when it's not worth it

If the fix is a process change, a better hire, or an off-the-shelf tool for $49/mo, we will tell you that on the fit call instead of scoping a custom system. 5 Cypress is the right fit when the automation genuinely pays for itself — not before.

A small network, not a large team

5 Cypress is intentionally small. When a project needs design, bookkeeping domain expertise, or AI research, I bring in trusted people from my network — and introduce them to you. No bloated team, no invisible subcontractors.
