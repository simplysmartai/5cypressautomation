Our Process

From manual chaos to automated system in 2 weeks. Here's exactly how we take your disconnected tools and turn them into one automated system.

01
Week 1

We Map Your Current Workflow

We watch how you currently handle orders, bookings, and customer data. Then we show you exactly where you're wasting time—usually copying info between Calendly, QuickBooks, and email.

02
Week 1

You See the Automation Plan

We show you exactly what will happen automatically. Example: "Customer books demo → QuickBooks quote created → confirmation email sent → team notified on Slack." You approve before we build anything.

03
Week 2

We Connect Your Tools

We link Calendly to QuickBooks, QuickBooks to ShipStation, everything to your CRM. You'll see test orders flow through automatically. We fix any issues before going live.

04
Week 2

You Go Live

Your team starts using the new system. Orders process automatically. Invoices create themselves. Customers get instant confirmations. You get 15 hours per week back.

05
Ongoing

We Monitor & Improve

We watch for errors and fix them before you notice. Every month, we show you new workflows we can automate. Your system gets better as your business grows.
