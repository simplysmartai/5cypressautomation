Common Questions

Everything you need to know about The Cypress Method.

How long does it really take?

Quick-Win Sprint delivers in 1 week. Automation Foundation takes 2 weeks. We don't start the clock until after your approval call—so you're never paying for planning time.

Do I own the workflows or are they yours?

You own everything. We give you admin access to all platforms (n8n, Make, Zapier). You can edit, export, or cancel anytime. No vendor lock-in.

What if my tools aren't on the list?

Most SaaS tools have APIs we can connect. If your tool has webhooks, Zapier support, or a REST API, we can automate it. We'll confirm compatibility on the discovery call.

What happens if something breaks?

All packages include 30-90 days of email support. Automation Partner retainer ($297/month) includes 24/7 monitoring—we fix errors before you notice them.

Can I add more workflows later?

Absolutely. Most clients start with Quick-Win Sprint, see the time savings, then add 2-3 more workflows. You can upgrade to Automation Partner retainer anytime for ongoing development.
