Three results from firms that had a clear, repeatable problem and the patience to let automation solve it properly. Clients are anonymized; numbers are real.

Dashboard Regional CPA Firm 6-partner firm · 2,200+ clients · Southeast US
Service: Practice KPI Dashboard →
The problem

Partners were running QBO reports manually twice a month and emailing screenshots to each other. No shared view. AR aging was tracked in a spreadsheet updated by one staff member. When that person left, the spreadsheet stopped being updated.

The solution

We built a live dashboard pulling from QBO and their Karbon instance — realization rate, WIP by partner, AR aging buckets, and billing trend. One URL, updated every 4 hours, accessible from any device.

Live in 12 business days

3.5 hrs

saved per partner per month on reporting

22%

reduction in overdue AR within 60 days of going live

0 hrs

staff time on manual spreadsheet updates

1 URL

every partner shares the same view, live

"We used to argue in Monday meetings about whose numbers were right. Now there's one source and everyone uses it."

— Managing Partner
Reporting Fractional CFO Practice 1 CFO · 14 SMB clients · Monthly close reporting
Service: Monthly Client Snapshot →
The problem

Every month, the CFO spent 6–8 hours building client reports: exporting QBO, reformatting in Excel, copying into a branded template, emailing. For 14 clients. The same 6–8 hours every month.

The solution

We automated the full chain. QBO close triggers generation. Each client gets a branded PDF report and a live web dashboard — auto-delivered before the 5th. The CFO reviews but does not rebuild.

Live in 10 business days

7 hrs

reclaimed every month on report generation

14 clients

each receiving branded, individualized reports

Day 3

reports delivered — before clients ask

2 new clients

added with zero additional reporting overhead

"I added two clients immediately after launch because I knew the reporting wasn't going to consume me. That's $2,400/month in new revenue directly traceable to this system."

— Fractional CFO, Founder
AI · Intake Tax & Bookkeeping Firm 3-person team · 380 individual tax clients
Service: Document Intake & AI Sort →
The problem

Tax season meant clients emailing documents to whoever they knew at the firm — sometimes to the owner's personal email, sometimes as 12 separate attachments. Staff spent the first three weeks of season just sorting and naming files.

The solution

We built a client-facing upload portal and connected it to OpenAI Vision for classification. Every uploaded document is identified, renamed to convention, and routed to the correct TaxDome job folder automatically.

Live in 16 business days

47 hrs

staff time saved in first tax season

<5 sec

per document: classified and filed

0

misfiled documents in first season (vs. 12+ prior year)

380 clients

each receiving a personal upload link with instructions

"The first week of tax season used to feel like triage. This year it felt like February."

— Firm Owner
