Gulf wet season: June–September, peak July–Aug. Driven by Gulf of Mexico moisture & afternoon convection.
Mobile & Pascagoula are the wettest (~65–66" annually). Houston is drier (~49") with higher year-to-year variability.
Tampa is the warmest city (~73°F avg) and second-driest (~46") with its own distinct wet season pulse.
