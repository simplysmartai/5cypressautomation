COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you're wondering.

How much does it cost?

Every project is scoped based on complexity and integrations. We don't use hourly billing — you get a fixed price before work starts so there are no surprises. Most projects for small businesses land in the range most SMBs spend on a single bad hire's first week of inefficiency.

How long does it take to build?

Most single-workflow projects are live in 2–4 weeks. Multi-system builds take 4–8 weeks. We scope it clearly upfront so you know the timeline before anything starts.

What tools and software do you work with?

We build on top of what you already use. Common integrations include QuickBooks, Stripe, Zoho CRM, Clio, HubSpot, Google Workspace, Calendly, Slack, and virtually any platform with an API. If you're not sure — bring it up on the fit call and we'll tell you.

Will this replace my staff?

No — it frees them. Automation takes over the repetitive, error-prone tasks so your team can focus on higher-value work. Our clients don't let staff go; they grow without needing to hire more.

What happens if something breaks?

Every system ships with a 30-day support window. We monitor for errors, and you have a direct line to us — not a ticket queue. We also document everything so you understand your own system and aren't dependent on us forever.

Do we own the code when it's done?

Yes. Full code ownership, full documentation, full handoff. We don't lock you into a subscription you can't escape. When the project is done, it's yours.
