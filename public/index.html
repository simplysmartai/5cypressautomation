Purpose-built for your practice.

Dashboard

Practice KPI Dashboard

Real-time realization rate, WIP, AR aging, and billing metrics — pulled from QBO + your practice management system and surfaced to every partner in one live panel.

Reporting

Monthly Client Snapshot

One click after QBO close: auto-generated PDF report + branded web dashboard per client, delivered before the 5th — no manual data pulls, no formatting.

AI · Intake

Document Intake & AI Sort

Clients upload to a shared portal. OpenAI Vision classifies each file and routes it to the right job in Karbon, TaxDome, or SharePoint — in seconds.

Onboarding

Engagement → Onboarding Flow

When a DocuSign engagement letter is signed, the system auto-creates the Karbon job, adds the client to QBO, and books their kickoff call via Calendly — hands-free.

Lead Recovery

Missed-Lead Text-Back

A prospect calls after hours or submits a form. They get a personalized text in under 60 seconds — before they move on to your competitor.

Close · Flagship

Month-End Close Accelerator

An agentic close engine that runs reconciliations, flags variances, drafts adjusting JEs for review, and pushes a partner-ready close package — same day QBO is locked.

AR · Cashflow

AR / Collections Agent

Aging invoices chased automatically with a graduated 15/30/45/60 day cadence — tone-matched to the client relationship and escalated to a partner only when needed.

Advisory · Forecast

Cashflow Forecast Agent

A 13-week rolling cashflow forecast per client, refreshed every morning from QBO actuals — with auto-alerts for low balance projections and covenant risks.
