1. Information We Collect

When you book a consultation or use our services, we collect:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Business information (company name, industry, tools you use)
  • Communication records (emails, support tickets)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our automation services
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Send relevant product updates (you can opt out anytime)

3. Data Security

We protect your data using industry-standard security measures. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

You have the right to:

  • Export your data
