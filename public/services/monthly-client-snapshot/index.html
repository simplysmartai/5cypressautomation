How it works

From kickoff to
live in 10–14 business days.

01

Map your report template

We document exactly what each client receives — balance sheet, P&L, KPIs, narrative summary — and build the generation logic once.

02

Wire the QBO trigger

The system listens for period close events in QBO. When the books are closed, generation starts automatically.

03

Generate PDF + dashboard

Reports are rendered with your firm branding, optionally enriched with AI narrative, and uploaded to both PDF and a live URL.

04

Review and send

Optional partner review step before delivery. One-click approve, then SendGrid routes each report to the right client contact.
