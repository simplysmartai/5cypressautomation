# Pricing Models — Premium SEO Skill Revenue Paths

Three proven business models for monetizing this skill. Choose based on your market + resources.

---

## Model 1: Direct Service (5 Cypress)

**What:** You generate SEO reports on 5cypress.com and sell them directly

**Revenue:**
- Price: $49/report
- Volume: 100-500 reports/month (target: 1000+)
- Monthly revenue: $4,900 - $24,500+

**Margin:**
- API costs: ~$1.50/report (DataForSEO $0.50 + OpenAI $0.01 + Stripe 2.9% fee)
- Gross margin: 97%
- Net margin (after hosting): ~75%

**Unit economics (1000 reports/month):**
```
Revenue: $49,000
API costs: $1,500
Stripe fees: $1,421 (2.9% + $0.30)
Hosting: $20-50/mo (Cloudflare)

Net profit: $45,979/month (94% margin)
```

**Strengths:**
- 100% revenue capture
- Build relationship with buyers (land-and-expand upsell opportunities)
- Direct feedback loop for product improvement
- Practice before selling to others

**Challenges:**
- Requires marketing/sales (how do you drive demand?)
- Customer support costs (questions, refunds)
- Dependent on your brand
- Scaling requires building trust individually

**How to acquire customers:**
1. Cold email to agency owners ("I run premium SEO audits for $49")
2. ProductHunt / Gumroad listing
3. Facebook/LinkedIn ads ($5-10/report CAC)
4. Referral loop (happy customers recommend)
5. Content marketing (write SEO guides, link to audit tool)

**Recommendation:** Start with this model. Run 100+ reports, validate demand, then consider white-label.

---

## Model 2: White-Label / Licensing

**What:** Other companies pay you a monthly fee to run reports on their domain (you host)

**Revenue:**
- Price: $15-25/month per agency client
- Example: 20 agencies × 5 clients each = 100 clients
- Monthly revenue: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Margin:**
- Each agency is responsible for selling end-client reports
- You keep $15-25/mo per client
- Report-level costs: $1.50/report (DataForSEO, OpenAI, Stripe)
- If each agency client runs 1-2 reports/month: cost = $50-100/month per 5-client agency
- Net per agency: $75-125-150/month (depending on report volume)

**Unit economics (20 agencies × 5 clients = 100 white-label clients):**
```
Revenue (licensing): $1,500-2,500/month
API costs: $100-300/month (depends on report volume)
Hosting: $50-100/month

Net profit: $1,100-2,050/month

Plus: Revenue share on reports if you handle Stripe
```

**Strengths:**
- Recurring revenue (subscription model)
- Less support load (agencies support their own clients)
- Scalable (20 agencies = 100 end-clients, no new code)
- Faster time-to-revenue than selling 100 individual reports

**Challenges:**
- Lower per-unit revenue than direct
- Agencies are competitive (won't pay if they can build it themselves)
- Support burden (agencies ask questions, want customization)
- Competition: Ahrefs, SEMrush sell white-label audits cheaper

**Pricing strategy:**
- **Starter:** $10/mo/client (agencies with 1-3 clients)
- **Growth:** $15/mo/client (agencies with 5-10 clients)
- **Enterprise:** $20-25/mo/client (agencies with 20+ clients or custom branding)

**How to acquire white-label customers:**
1. Cold email to 50 agencies ("Want premium SEO audits on your domain? $15/mo per client")
2. Agency partner platforms (PartnerStack, JoinBonus, Zapier)
3. Pitches at marketing conferences
4. Referral from existing customers

**Recommendation:** After proving direct sales (Model 1), offer white-label to top 10 customers.

## Model 3: Self-Serve SaaS / Marketplace

**What:** Agencies buy a one-time license, deploy on their own domain

**Revenue:**
- Price: $299-499 (one-time)
- Volume: 50-200 licenses/year
- Annual revenue: $14,950 - $99,800

**Margin:**
- You don't run any reports
- You don't pay API costs
- Support: ~20% of revenue (documentation, setup help, GitHub issues)
- Net margin: ~70%

**Unit economics (100 licenses/year @ $399):**
```
Revenue: $39,900
Support costs: $8,000 (200 hours @ $40/hr)
Hosting (documentation): $50/month = $600

Net profit: $31,300/year (78% margin)

Plus: Optional revenue share if you offer managed hosting
```

**Strengths:**
- True passive income (not tied to reports run)
- Lowest support burden
- Infinitely scalable
- Agencies can deploy immediately (no dependency on your infrastructure)

**Challenges:**
- Requires excellent documentation/setup guide
- Tier-one support (first-time deployments are tricky)
- Hard to differentiate from competitors
- Lower per-unit revenue than Models 1 or 2

**Pricing strategy:**
- **Solo Agency License:** $299 (1 agency, own domain)
- **Team License:** $599 (1 company, unlimited domains/subdomains)
- **White-Label License:** $999 (permission to rebrand and resell)

**How to acquire self-serve customers:**
1. Gumroad / Lemonsqueezy listing
2. GitHub releases (make the repo public)
3. SEO / marketing communities (DigitalMarketer, Indie Hackers, ProductHunt)
4. YouTube tutorials ("How to run premium SEO audits on your own domain")
5. Reddit posts on r/SEO, r/webdev, r/devops

**Recommendation:** After white-label, package setup docs and sell on marketplace (lowest ongoing effort).

## Hybrid Model (Recommended)

**Combine all three for maximum revenue:**

```
Month 1-3: Direct service (Model 1)
- Sell $49 reports on 5cypress.com
- Target: 100 reports = $4,900 revenue

Month 4-6: White-label (Model 2)
- Sell $15/mo/client to 10 agencies
- Recurring: $750/mo + report commissions
- Target: 50 white-label clients = $750/mo recurring

Month 7+: Self-serve (Model 3)
- Sell $399 licenses on marketplace
- Passive: revenue depends on marketing
- Target: 50/year = ~$20k/year income

Year 1 blended revenue:
- Direct: $20,000 (ramping down as you focus on white-label)
- White-label: $9,000 (6 months × $750 + 6 months × $1,500)
- Self-serve: ~$2,000 (first 5 licenses)
- Total: ~$31,000

Year 2 blended:
- Direct: $30,000 (1,000+ reports/year, passive once optimized)
- White-label: $18,000 (growing to 100+ clients)
- Self-serve: $15,000 (50+ licenses/year + word-of-mouth)
- Total: ~$63,000

Year 3:
- Direct: $60,000
- White-label: $36,000 (true recurring revenue)
- Self-serve: $40,000 (network effects)
- Total: ~$136,000

```
